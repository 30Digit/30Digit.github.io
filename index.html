How we work

Discuss your needs

We'll explore your business or work either over the phone, or in person (perhaps with tea and cake!). By the end of the meeting we'll have a thorough understanding of your requirements and how a website can best help you.

Proposal & draft designs

Next, we'll send you a comprehensive proposal which includes a description of the work to be done, timescales, price and terms and conditions. Once confirmed, we'll work from your feedback until you are happy that you have the website you want.

Make it happen

We sit hunched over computers coding your website. Then you will receive an email with a link to your final design for you to see and approve. Let us know any final tweaks and - hey presto - your website will be live on the internet. We will provide an easy-to-follow guide on how to update new website.

About

30 Digit is a family run website design business based in Bath. We design and develop bespoke websites and promotional materials to fit organisations' and individuals' specific needs - all at an affordable price.

With experience in both redevelopment of existing websites or launching an organisation's very first website and branding, we ensure that we understand your business. This means we design a website that enables you to communicate your vision and passion effectively to your customers.
